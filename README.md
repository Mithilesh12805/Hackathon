## ðŸ“Œ Problem Statement

Citizens and students in India face multiple challenges while accessing government schemes and public opportunities:

- Information is scattered across multiple government portals  
- Content is written in complex bureaucratic language  
- Eligibility criteria are unclear  
- Required documents and steps are confusing  
- Language barriers exclude non-English speakers  

These issues result in **low awareness and underutilization** of government benefits.

## ðŸ’¡ Proposed Solution: Bharat Setu

Bharat Setu addresses these challenges by providing an **AI-powered guidance platform** that allows users to ask questions via **text or voice** and receive:

- Simple explanations in citizen-friendly language  
- Clear eligibility criteria  
- Step-by-step application or process guidance  
- Required document lists with explanations  

The platform is designed to be accessible even for users with **low digital literacy or limited internet connectivity**.
